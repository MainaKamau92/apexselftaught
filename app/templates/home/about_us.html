About The Site

Apex Self Taught aims at being an oasis in a desert of regard for formal accreditation over competence.

Apex believes that the sum competence of a professional does not reside solely in formal structured academia. We envision a future where the roles of competence, intellectualism and professionalism are considered outside the formality of diploma certificates.

We believe that self-drive to pursue one's passion should not be rewarded with disregard but rather optimisms and strides of affirmation. We do not presume to have the silver bullet in dealing with work incompetence or offer presumed guarantees in preferred cultural fits, but we ensure that the match is a as close.

AST takes a professional first approach, in that as much as employers are an integral part in ensuring the site is possible, we mainly aim at ensuring self-taught professionals have an equal competitive leverage as their diplomas wielding counterparts. We aim at giving them a chance to have a face to face interview from where they can prove their worth.

In a 2018 Hackerank survey more than 65% of the students were self-taught programmers and the number is expected to rise with time. There being no shortage of online resources that make it incredibly efficient to acquire specific set of skills that put one at the foe front of their desired field, we wanted to ensure that these professionals do not toil in vain.

What we are not:

We are not a recruitment firm we do not take any responsibilities in training the professionals on this site. We do not offer any guarantees outside the periphery of the resumes that have been posted by the developers and designers on the site.

We do not guarantee jobs for the professionals on this site either we merely provide a platform for hirers to get a pool of professionals with hands on experience and of their own volition chose to engage further with the said individuals.
