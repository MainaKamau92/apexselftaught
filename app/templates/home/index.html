Apex Self Taught

AST is an idea that aims at creating a site that gives self taught professionals in the fields of design and software engineering a chance to get hired by firms without any formal certification. It is based of the belief that getting a degree, diploma and the like however important it may be should not suffice as reason enough to exercise monopoly over said fields It's an ambitious project, completely open source.

Professionals

Apex provides the chance for self-taught professionals in the fields of software engineering and design with a chance to get that chance for a formal interview without formal accreditation acting as a barrier. AST gives the candidates on the site a chance to sell their level of skill and expertise.

Hirers

AST gives employers an easier time in shortlisting potential candidates for a job by according them direct access to not only an updated resume but a taste of the caliber of work the professionals on the site can and has performed.
