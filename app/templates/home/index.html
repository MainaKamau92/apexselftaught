Apex Self Taught

Apex is an idea that aims at creating a site that gives self taught professionals in the fields of design and software engineering a chance to get hired by firms without any formal certification. It is based of the belief that getting a degree, diploma and the like however important it may be should not suffice as reason enough to exercise monopoly over said fields It's an ambitious project, completely open source.
